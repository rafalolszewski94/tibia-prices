How it works

Calculate profitability instantly without leaving the auction page.

Instant Conversion

PLN prices appear automatically next to Tibia Coin values on auction lists and detail pages. No extra clicks required.

Your Rate, Your Device

Market prices fluctuate. You set the "Price per 250 TC" in the extension popup. The page updates immediately to reflect your custom rate.

Lightweight & Focused

Runs exclusively on tibia.com/charactertrade. It doesn't use resources on other tabs and has zero impact on browser performance.

Privacy by Default

We believe simple tools shouldn't require your data. Tibia Prices is built to be completely transparent and local.

No Data Collection

The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any analytics or personal usage data to external servers.

Local Storage Only

Your custom currency conversion rate is stored locally in your browser (Local Storage) and never leaves your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the conversion rate?

Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar. A popup will appear where you can enter the current price of 250 TC in PLN. Click "Save", and the Tibia trade page will update instantly.

Does this work on other websites?

No. The extension is strictly limited to run only on www.tibia.com/charactertrade/*. It remains dormant on all other websites to ensure performance and privacy.

Is this official?

No, this is a third-party tool developed by the community to help players visualize real-money values. It is not affiliated with CipSoft or Tibia.
